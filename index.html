30-40% OF PRODUCE IS WASTED

While offices settle for generic, highly-processed packaged foods, up to 20.7 million tonnes in Tanzania (annually) of fresh produce is lost globally.

We saw an opportunity to derive value and defy decay. By rescuing surplus that would have gone to waste, we repurpose these food items and create premium, shelf-stable bites that fuel your team, support local farmers, and protect the planet.
